Syzygy: Basic Papers

An early Syzygy version, as used in our lab's VR Navigation experiment, was available for download from the web in September 2000. Since then, the code base has matured and is still under active development, changing as our experience working with PC clusters deepens. These papers provide a good overview of the design decisions made at early stages in Syzygy's life and explore the motivations for those decisions.

  • "Networking and Management Frameworks for Cluster-Based Graphics"
  • "Syzygy: Native PC Cluster VR"
  • "Tele-sports and Tele-dance: Full Body Network Interaction"
  • "Myriad: Scalable VR via Peer-to-Peer Connectivity, PC Clustering, and Transient Inconsistency"
